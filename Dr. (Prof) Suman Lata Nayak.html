ABOUT DOCTOR

Dr. (Prof) Suman Lata Nayak is a very reputed Nephrologist practicing successfully for more than 20 years.
Her area of specialization covers Kidney Transplantation and treatment of Hepatitis in patients with Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD), Tuberculosis and Bone disease.
She is also well known as a Nephro-Pathologist and is certified by ISN.
She played a leading role in setting up the Department of Nephrology in Institute of Liver and Biliary Sciences (ILBS), New Delhi.
She was awarded Distinction in ISN-AINO Nephro –Pathology course and TANCRIT 2013 at ILBS.
She takes keen interest in teaching and chairs the post of Assistant Professor in ILBS.
She is actively involved in the mission to spread kidney awareness among people and organises Free Camp on World Kidney Day every year.
Dr. Suman Lata regularly takes part in Workshops and Conferences.
She enjoys the membership of the prestigious association International Society of Nephrology (ISN).
She engages herself in research work and has several publications in National and International Journals to her credit.
