ABOUT DOCTOR

Dr A B Govindaraj is a well-known Orthopedecian and a joint replacement surgeon with an experience of more than 30 years.
He has expertise in Orthopedic Surgery, Joint Replacement Surgery, Spine and Joint Replacement, Spine Surgery, Hip Replacement SurgeryKnee Surgery, and Pediatric Orthopedics.
He also has command over the treatment of ACL (Anterior Cruciate Ligament) Surgery, Hip Resurfacing, Shoulder replacement, and Instrumentation spine surgery.
Dr. Govindaraj is an active member of the Indian Orthopedic Association - LM 1561, American Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons - 151329, Bombay Orthopaedic Society, Association of Surgeons of India, and Tamil Nadu Orthopedic Association.
He has presented many papers in international as well as national conferences and attended several seminars.
