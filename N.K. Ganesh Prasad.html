ABOUT DOCTOR

Dr. N.K. Ganesh Prasad is a renowned Nephrologist and Renal Specialist.
He has nearly 29 years of experience in the field of Nephrology.
After completing his MBBS from Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Belgaum in 1991, he completed his DNB in General Medicine from DNB Board, New Delhi in 1996.
Further, he did DNB in Nephrology from Sathya Sai Institue of Higher Medical Science, Puttaparthy in 2002.
He has been honored with several awards and recognitions.
He is an active member of the Indian Society of Nephrology (ISN), International Society of Nephrology (ISN), American Society of Nephrology, Nephrology Association of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry and Indian Society of Peritoneal Dialysis.
