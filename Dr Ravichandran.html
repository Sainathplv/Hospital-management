Dr. Ravichandran G is a dermatologist with 32+ years of experience.

He is a member of IADVL, Association of Cutaneous Surgeons India, Indian Medical Association and Indian Association of Dermatologists.
He has many presentations and publications to his credit at the National and International levels.
