Dr Pallav S. Kishanpuria



ABOUT DOCTOR

Dr. Pallav S. Kishanpuria is a coveted Dermatologist at BLK Super Specialty Hospital, New Delhi.
He has 16 years of experience as a Dermatologist, Venereologist, Pediatric Dermatologist, and Dermatosurgeon.
He was honored with 'Doctor of the Year' in 2015.
He is an active member of the Indian Association of Dermatologists, Venereologists, and Leprologists (IADVL).
He owns a private clinic, named Agrawal skin ortho & psychiatry clinic, Janakpuri.
He has several papers and publications in national and international journals.
