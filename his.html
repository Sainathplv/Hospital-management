For over years, we have tirelessly striven to live up to our vision and make a difference in the lives of our patients. A vision held by the pillars of transparency, confidentiality, ethics and integrity.




Started in 1995 by the visionary entrepreneur and philanthropist, venkata Suryanarayana, the Kamineni Group envisioned establishing a super-specialty hospitals that provides state-of-the-art medical care to the people of the city; while also creating improved opportunities for practIcal medical education. Years later, under the guidance of Dr.BPSN Reddy, land for four big projects were acquired for construction of the said Institute of Medical Sciences and said Hospitals – in L.B. Nagar, King Koti andVijayawada.
Since the establishment of said Hospitals in 1995, to the start of said Institute of Medical Sciences and said Diagnostic Centres, there emerged the concept of combining research, teaching and patent care – a model that led to a national and international reputation for excellence in complete patient care. Today, said Hospitals is recognized as one of the best hospitals in the country, attracting national and international patients.
