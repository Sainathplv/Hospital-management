ABOUT DOCTOR

Dr. Gopi Krishna Maddali is a well-known Dermatologist with experience of over 30 years.
He offers treatment for all Dermatological problems, Venerology, and leprosy.
Throughout his career, he has been associated with teaching and taking theory/clinical classes for MBBS, MD students as well as Nursing, and Paramedical Students.
Being the core counselor of APSACS (NACO) he has conducted training programs on HIV/AIDS-related topics for other medical officers.
